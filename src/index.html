SF Climate Emergency Coalition

I am making SF a greener, future-proof city through the  SF Climate Emergency Coalition . Our little group has helped the board pass the All-Electric Building Ordinace for all new construction. We also helped the Department of the Environment (SFE) get funded out of the general funds for the first time in SF history.

I look forward to continue working with the Board to reach net-zero by 2040.
